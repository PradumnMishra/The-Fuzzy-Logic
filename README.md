# The-Fuzzy-Logic
Power BI Portfolio Project
📌 Project Overview
This project features a comprehensive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze the financial health and product lifecycle of a toy retail business. The analysis covers $1.94M in Gross Sales across a three-year period, transforming raw transactional data into actionable insights regarding revenue growth, product affinity, and launch impact.

🚀 Key Features & Functionality
Executive Sales Overview: High-level tracking of Gross Sales, COGS, Net Revenue, and Gross Profit Margins.

Time-Series Analysis: Monthly and Quarterly revenue trends showing a 171.77% YoY growth surge.

Market Basket Analysis: A dedicated "Product Pairs" matrix identifying which items are most frequently bought together to inform bundling strategies.

Product Lifecycle Tracking: A "Revenue Shift" waterfall analysis measuring the incremental impact of new product launches on the existing portfolio.

Customer Behavior Segmentation: Tracking the transition from "Single Order" to "Multiple Order" customers over time.

📊 Key Business Insights
1. Explosive Revenue Growth
The business scaled from a baseline in 2012 to a peak of $1.94M Total Revenue. Notably, Q4 2014 was the highest-performing quarter ($364.56K), suggesting a strong holiday seasonality that requires proactive inventory planning.

2. The "Hero Product" Risk
'The Original Mr. Fuzzy' is the primary revenue driver, accounting for 62.47% of total revenue share. While a strong performer, this represents a high dependency. The dashboard highlights the need to diversify the product mix.

3. Successful Product Expansion
The launch of 'The Hudson River Mini Bear' was highly effective, resulting in a 44.2% positive revenue shift. Conversely, 'The Forever Love Bear' saw a -6.8% shift, suggesting it may have cannibalized sales from 'The Original Mr. Fuzzy'.

4. Cross-Selling Opportunities
Data shows that 'The Original Mr. Fuzzy' and 'The Birthday Sugar Panda' are the most frequent pair (2,036 co-occurrences). This insight allows the marketing team to create targeted "Buy Both" discount bundles to increase Average Order Value (AOV).
